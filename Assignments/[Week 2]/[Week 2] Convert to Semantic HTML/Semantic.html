A Brief History

Austin has a rich history informed by its original inhabitants, geographic location, and key events that shaped the city.

Early Inhabitants

The earliest inhabitants were hunter-gatherers dating back to 9200 BC. Later Native American tribes included the Tonkawa, Comanches and Lipan Apaches.

Geographic Location

Austin is located at the intersection of the Balcones Escarpment and the Colorado River, which has influenced development.
