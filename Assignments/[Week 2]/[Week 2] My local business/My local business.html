KIMI - Cafè & Cake House

Kimi Restaurant, the place known around town for having the best desserts and cakes in the city. As soon as you walk through the doors of Kimi, you'll be enveloped in the sweet aromas of fresh baked goods and chocolate. It's a haven for those with a sugar craving Kimi's display case is piled high with rows of luscious, decadent cakes, cheesecakes, fruit tarts, cookies, and more. Whether you prefer chocolate, vanilla, strawberry or a swirl of all three, you'll find a perfectly crafted cake to fulfill your wildest confectionary dreams.
